A salon by Brittany

Small chair, big heart.

Salon 45:11 is a private studio in downtown Somonauk where every appointment gets unhurried, one-on-one attention. Brittany has been behind the chair for over fourteen years — coloring, cutting, and caring for clients from toddlers to grandparents.

This is the kind of salon where the music isn’t too loud, the conversation is yours to lead, and your hair is the only thing on the schedule. Kids are welcome. Special needs accommodations are offered without a second thought. And if you can’t make it in, Brittany comes to you.

“See you in the chair.” — Brittany, Owner & Stylist
